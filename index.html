About the Event

We are happy to inform you that, the faculty members of Speech Research Group (SRG), Department of CSE, Koneru Lakshmaiah Education Foundation, Vaddeswaram - 522302, AP, India, are hosting the Winter School on Speech and Audio Processing (WiSSAP-2024) on the theme of “Multilingual Speech Processing of Indic Languages” during December 09-12, 2024.

The Winter School on Speech and Audio Processing (WiSSAP) is an advanced school, organized annually in India since 2006. It provides a forum for researchers to enhance their expertise by exposing themselves to areas at the forefront of speech and audio signal processing. The past 16 WiSSAPs have covered different aspects of speech and audio processing perception, recognition, coding, enhancement, synthesis, spatialization, production, hearing aids, music etc. The past WiSSAPs were hosted by IISC Bangalore, IIT Chennai, Kanpur, Bombay, Guwahati, Mandi, IIIT Hyderabad, DAIICT Gandhinagar, SSN College Chennai, College of Engineering Thiruvananthapuram. The history of the WiSSAP is available at https://wissap2017.wixsite.com/wissap2017/history

The WISSAP comprises invited talks and tutorials from eminent researchers from India and abroad, presentation by research scholars and IT Industry. Researchers in academic, government and industrial organizations actively participate in this winter school.

The eminent researchers in the area of speech and audio processing are welcome to attend this school which provides significant insights and research directions.
